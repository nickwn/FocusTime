Welcome to Focus Time!

If you have caught yourself spending too much time on your phone, or if you want to learn a new skill or spend more time studying, but have a hard time sitting down and concentrating on the single task, Focus Time is for you. Use Focus Time to improve your focus and concentration. Unlike other mindfulness or focus apps, Focus Time allows you to set your own goals and track your progress, so you can train yourself to be a more focused person.



How to Use Focus Time

Using Focus Time is easy.

  • To create your first task, tap the plus on the bottom right corner. After filling in the info, tap save event.
  • You can see the time, goal, and reward and consequence you set for youself to remind yourself why you are spending this time.
  • You'll be able to see the goal you created on your home screen.
  • Tap start session to begin the timer. You won't see the number changing with the time because we don't want you to keep checking for the time to be over.
  • After the time is up, the button to end session will appear. Tap end session, and you have completed your goal for that day.
